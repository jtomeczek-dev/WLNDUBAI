AI Tools from the Conference

In the first video "From Fear to Strategy", we explored four powerful AI tools that transform legal work

Microsoft Copilot

Microsoft Copilot is an advanced AI-powered assistant integrated into the Microsoft 365 ecosystem, designed to automate legal drafting, analyze data, and streamline practice management.

Visit Tool

Google Gemini

Google Gemini is an advanced multimodal artificial intelligence system, integrated within Google Workspace, designed to assist legal professionals in document analysis, automated legal drafting, and rapid information retrieval.

Visit Tool

Claude.ai

Claude is a sophisticated AI model developed by Anthropic, featuring an exceptionally large context window that enables precise analysis of voluminous legal case files, contract consistency checks, and ethical support for legal decision-making processes.

Visit Tool

Perplexity.ai

Perplexity AI is an advanced AI-powered search engine that combines generative language models with real-time web access, providing legal professionals with precise answers supported by direct citations to source materials and legal authorities.
